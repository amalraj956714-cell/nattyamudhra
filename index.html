About Nattyamudhra

Nattyamudhra is a classical dance training center dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich heritage of Indian dance. We offer training in various dance forms including Mohiniyattam, Kerala Nadanam, Kathakali, Bharatanatyam, Kuchipudi, Folk Dance, and even Western styles. Our teaching follows the traditional Kerala Kalamandalam methods, ensuring authenticity and depth in every performance.

Ottanthullal

Kuchipudi

Graceful footwork and storytelling.

Mohiniyattam

Mohiniyattam is a graceful classical dance of Kerala, celebrated for its gentle swaying movements and expressive storytelling.

Keralanadanam

Kerala Nadanam is a modern classical dance form of Kerala that blends traditional temple dance techniques with expressive storytelling and graceful movements.

Western Dance

Western dance is a broad term for dance styles from the West, including genres like jazz, hip-hop, ballet, and contemporary, focusing on rhythm, creativity, and performance.

Folk Dance

Folk dance is a traditional dance form that reflects the culture, customs, and stories of a community, often performed during festivals and celebrations.
